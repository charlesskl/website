RC
Manufacturing overview

Our most complex category. Mechanical meets electronic. Concept to certified product.

01

In-house SMT electronics

Our dedicated Surface Mount Technology department handles circuit board assembly and electronic integration internally — giving us full control over motor performance, control responsiveness, and consistency across every unit we ship.

02

Structural engineering for play durability

Years of RC vehicle production have refined how we design and reinforce chassis and body structures. Built to withstand real-world play, our vehicles are stress-tested for the handling and impact children's RC toys demand.

03

Full range capability

From simple forward/reverse models to advanced multi-function vehicles with lights, sound, and complex control systems — our combined SMT, plastic molding, and automation capabilities let us handle the full RC spectrum under one roof.
