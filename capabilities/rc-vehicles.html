Overview

Our most technically complex category, engineered for performance and compliance.

We offer comprehensive OEM and ODM manufacturing services for remote control toy cars, covering basic to advanced functional models. Entered in 2016, RC vehicle manufacturing represents our highest-complexity category, requiring strong capabilities in structural engineering, electronic integration, and safety certification.

Our focus is on structural strength, precise control performance, and electronic safety, ensuring every model we produce meets the requirements of US and EU importers and retailers.

🔌

Electronic Integration

Transmitter and receiver electronics designed and integrated, covering 2.4GHz and IR control systems with tested signal range and reliability.

⚙

Structural Engineering

Chassis design and stress testing to meet durability and safety drop standards required for children's toy certification in target markets.

⚡

Battery and Power Safety

Compliance with battery safety regulations covering charging systems, overcharge protection, and CE/FCC/ASTM requirements.

✓

US and EU Electronic Safety Compliance

Full compliance with FCC, CE, and relevant ASTM F963 and EN 71 clauses covering electronic toy safety and RF emissions.
