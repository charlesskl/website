Overview

OEM & ODM RC vehicles,
built to perform.

Photo

RC vehicle manufacturing represents our highest-complexity category — requiring the integration of mechanical design, electronics, and plastic manufacturing. We support full OEM and ODM development, from motor and receiver integration through body design, battery management, and compliance certification for US and EU markets.

Photo
Photo

  • Electronics Integration

    Full integration of motor, receiver, transmitter, and battery systems — designed for stable control performance and safety.

  • Structural Engineering

    Chassis and body design engineered for durability under normal play conditions, with drop and impact testing at scale.

  • OEM & ODM Capability

    Supports both client-supplied designs (OEM) and our own original model development (ODM) — flexible for any product requirement.

  • Full Compliance Certification

    FCC (US), CE (EU), ASTM F963, and EN 71 compliance — full documentation package available for import requirements.

Manufacturing partner to the world's leading toy brands since 1987. Five product categories, four production sites, one consistent standard — US and EU compliant.
