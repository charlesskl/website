Overview

Interactive electronics,
built into every toy.

Electronic toys demand a seamless integration of electronics and plastics. Our team co-engineers the PCB layout, speaker placement, LED integration, and button mechanics alongside the plastic housing — producing interactive toys that perform consistently at scale, with full documentation for US and EU import compliance.

  • 🔊

    Sound & Music Integration

    Custom audio chips, speakers, and volume control systems integrated directly into the plastic toy body during manufacturing.

  • 💡

    Light & LED Systems

    LED strip, point-light, and flashing systems integrated with the plastic structure — tested for consistent output across production.

  • 📐

    Interactive Electronics

    Button, sensor, and touch-activated systems co-designed with the plastic housing for intuitive child interaction.

  • ✓

    CE & ASTM F963 / EN 71

    All electronic components tested and certified to CE (EU) and ASTM F963 / EN 71 (US/EU) standards. Full test reports available.
