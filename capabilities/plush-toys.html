Overview

High-quality plush for every market and application.

Our plush figures are widely used for gifts, branded merchandise, and licensed IP products. We operate full in-house soft toy production lines including fabric cutting, stuffing, sewing, and quality inspection. Every stage is managed under strict quality protocols to meet the requirements of European and American markets.

We have extensive experience producing plush to licensed character specifications with tight colour matching, finishing accuracy, and brand fidelity. Our material sourcing is fully documented and traceable to support compliance requirements.
