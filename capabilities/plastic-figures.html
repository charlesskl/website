Overview

Precision plastic manufacturing from concept to finished product.

We specialise in plastic toy manufacturing with strong in-house capabilities in mold development and injection molding. Our products include character figures and functional toys, produced in strict compliance with international toy safety standards. Customisation based on customer-provided designs is fully supported.

Our engineering team works directly with client artwork to develop tooling and produce samples before moving to mass production. Every mold developed is owned by the client and managed within our facility.

⚙

In-House Mold Development

Full mold design, tooling, and steel mold fabrication with no outsourcing — faster turnaround and tighter quality control.

🔧

Precision Injection Molding

Multi-shot and single-shot injection molding with tight dimensional tolerances for consistent, high-quality output at scale.

🎨

Custom Artwork and Finishing

Full painting, pad printing, tampo printing, and assembly finishing based on client colour and artwork specifications.

✓

Safety Compliance

Manufactured in strict compliance with ASTM F963 (US) and EN 71 (EU) toy safety standards. Third-party testing supported.
