From first sample to final carton

Every stage of production happens under one roof — from initial 3D printed prototypes through injection molding, painting, assembly, and outbound carton packing. No handoffs, no third-party delays, full visibility throughout.

Whether your product is a simple figure or a multi-component functional toy, our integrated manufacturing setup is designed to scale efficiently — keeping lead times tight and costs predictable as order volumes grow.
