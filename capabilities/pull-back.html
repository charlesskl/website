Overview

Character-led pull-back toys built for durability and consistent performance.

Pull-back figures combine character designs with reliable pull-back mechanisms. We provide structural optimisation and efficient mass production based on customer artwork, ensuring consistent durability, safety, and quality across every production run.

Our engineering team validates mechanism performance and durability before production sign-off, testing spring tension, drive consistency, and housing strength to meet applicable ASTM and EN 71 standards.
