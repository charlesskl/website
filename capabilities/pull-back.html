Overview

Precision mechanisms,
character-driven design.

Pull-back toys require a tight integration of mechanical design and plastic manufacturing. Our team manages the full process — from translating client artwork into tooling through mechanism calibration and mass production. Each unit is tested for durability and consistent pull-back performance before shipment.

  • ⚙

    Integrated Mechanism Design

    Pull-back mechanism designed and integrated directly with the plastic figure body for optimal performance and longevity.

  • 🎨

    Character Design Support

    Client artwork translated into production-ready tooling — supporting licensed and original character designs.

  • 🔧

    Durability Testing

    Each product batch tested for pull-back mechanism durability and consistent performance across mass production runs.

  • ✓

    Safety Compliance

    Produced in compliance with ASTM F963 and EN 71 standards — suitable for US and EU retail markets.
