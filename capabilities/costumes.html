Overview

Licensed & custom costumes,
crafted with care.

Photo

Our costume production lines handle fabric cutting, sewing, embellishment, and finishing — all in-house. We produce character and themed dress-up costumes for licensed IP holders and custom brand clients, with full compliance to US and EU toy and textile standards.

Photo
Photo

  • In-House Fabric Production

    Dedicated cutting and sewing lines for costume construction, from pattern to finished garment with no outsourcing.

  • Licensed IP Expertise

    Experienced in producing officially licensed character costumes with strict adherence to brand guidelines and approval workflows.

  • Embellishment & Detailing

    Screen printing, embroidery, and accessory attachment for high-detail costume finishing.

  • Safety & Compliance

    All fabrics and trims tested to EN 71 and ASTM F963. CE marking and flammability compliance supported.

Manufacturing partner to the world's leading toy brands since 1987. Five product categories, four production sites, one consistent standard — US and EU compliant.
