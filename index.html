About Us

Built Over Decades

From a single workshop in Sha Tau Kok to a globally expanding manufacturing group — our growth reflects a relentless commitment to quality, capacity, and craftsmanship.

A Manufacturing Legacy Since 1987

Royal Regent was established in 1987 in Sha Tau Kok, Hong Kong — a modest beginning that would define decades of manufacturing excellence. Driven by consistent investment in capacity, technology, and product diversification, we have evolved into a vertically integrated OEM toy manufacturer with operations spanning mainland China and Southeast Asia.

Today, our factories in Dongguan, Heyuan, Hunan, and Indonesia give us the scale, resilience, and flexibility to meet the demands of international brands and retailers. Every production line we operate — whether for plastic figures, plush toys, remote control vehicles, or electronic toys — is built to meet the stringent safety standards of the US and EU markets.

We do not just manufacture toys. We build lasting partnerships with our clients by combining technical expertise, transparent communication, and an unwavering focus on quality at every stage of production.

1987 — Founded
Established in Sha Tau Kok, marking the official beginning of our manufacturing journey.
1990 — Longhua Expansion
Relocated to Longhua, significantly expanding production scale to meet growing demand.
1997 — Dongguan Operations
Expanded to Dongguan to accommodate rapidly growing market demand.
2000 — Soft Toy Entry
Commissioned the Dongguan factory expansion, launching plush toy and costume production lines — our first entry into soft toy manufacturing.
2003 — Heyuan Footprint
Extended operations to Heyuan, establishing a multi-location structure to strengthen supply chain resilience.
2005 — Capacity Upgrade
Completed the Heyuan factory expansion, supporting product line diversification and increased capacity.
2010 — Doll Production
Launched doll production lines, integrating sewing craftsmanship with plastic molding technology.
2016 — RC Vehicles
Entered remote control vehicle manufacturing, building capabilities in structural complexity and electronic integration.
2018 — Hunan Expansion
Expanded production to Hunan, significantly increasing overall manufacturing capacity.
2023 — Indonesia Entry
Officially entered the Indonesian market — a key milestone in our global expansion and supply chain diversification strategy.
2025 — Indonesia Phase II
Phase II of the Indonesia factory commenced operations, further expanding regional production capacity and international market responsiveness.
