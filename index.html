About Us

Our Story

From a small workshop to a global toy manufacturer — our passion for play has driven every step of the journey.

Building a Legacy of Play

Royal Regent was founded with a simple belief: every child deserves toys that are safe, creative, and built to last. What started as a dream has grown into a trusted name in toy manufacturing.

Today, we combine cutting-edge manufacturing processes with timeless design principles to create products that parents trust and children love. Our commitment to quality and safety remains at the heart of everything we do.

Founded
Royal Regent established with a vision to redefine toy manufacturing standards.
First Product Line
Launched our first collection of educational toys, winning industry recognition.
Global Expansion
Expanded operations internationally, serving markets across Asia, Europe, and the Americas.
Innovation Hub
Opened state-of-the-art R&D center focused on sustainable and smart toy design.
Today
Continuing to set the standard in toy safety, creativity, and manufacturing excellence.
