Our Story

38 years of
craftsmanship.

From a single workshop in Sha Tau Kok, Hong Kong, Royal Regent has grown into a multi-site OEM and ODM toy manufacturer trusted by leading global brands — delivering safe, compliant, and precision-engineered toys from China and Indonesia to markets worldwide.

We believe great manufacturing is built on trust, not just transactions. For 38 years, we have partnered with brands to turn creative visions into finished products — with the craftsmanship, compliance, and consistency their markets demand.
